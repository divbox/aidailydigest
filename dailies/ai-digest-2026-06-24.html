Sakana Fugu: Multi-Agent Routing Model Targets Frontier Without Export Control Risk
Sakana AI launched Fugu, a routing model that dispatches each request to a pool of specialist models and stitches the results back together through a single API — hiding the multi-agent complexity from callers. It ships in two tiers: Fugu (fast, everyday coding and chat) and Ultra (patent research, security testing). Sakana explicitly pitches it as "frontier capability without the risk of export controls," responding to Anthropic's Mythos and Fable models being banned. Early reception is mixed: users report the model doesn't consistently perform at frontier level, and there's skepticism about the model mix and cost claims.
