The Thread Worth Pulling

Claude Tag is the story today. Anthropic just moved Claude from a tool you open to a participant in your existing work environment. The pattern: first Claude Code brought AI into the terminal for individual devs, then Cowork brought it to desktop files, now Claude Tag brings it into team communication. The natural next step is email, calendar, and project management — if Anthropic keeps this pace, "AI coworker" becomes literal within a year.

Also worth watching: Fugu's export-control routing is a clever architectural response to geopolitics. If this pattern catches on, the model layer becomes a commodity and the routing/orchestration layer becomes the moat. OpenRouter already plays in this space — the race to own "the universal model API" is getting crowded.
